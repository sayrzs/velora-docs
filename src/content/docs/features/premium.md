## Activating Premium

Premium is applied **per server**. After purchasing, use the enable-premium script or contact support to activate it on your guild ID.

Once active, the **Premium** badge appears next to your server name in the dashboard sidebar.

---

## Trial

New servers get a **7-day free trial** of the Pro plan. No credit card required. After the trial, the server reverts to the Free plan unless upgraded.
